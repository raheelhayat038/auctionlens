# 🚗 AuctionLens PK
**The Ultimate AI-Powered Japanese Auction Sheet Scanner for Pakistan.**

AuctionLens PK is a specialized tool designed for car importers and buyers in Pakistan. It uses advanced AI to instantly translate, analyze, and verify Japanese auction sheets, helping users avoid "tempered" sheets and accidental vehicles.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **🔍 Instant AI Scan:** Upload or photograph an auction sheet to get an immediate summary in English.
* **🛠️ Tech Breakdown:** Automatic detection of Chassis numbers, Auction Grades (4.5, 4, R, etc.), and Interior/Exterior conditions.
* **📋 Professional Reports:** Generates a branded "Expert Report" with a verdict on the vehicle's health.
* **📥 Save & Share:** One-click "Download as Image" feature to share verified reports on OLX, PakWheels, or WhatsApp.
* **🌐 Live Auction Portal:** Direct access to live Japanese auction inventory via the integrated hub.
* **💬 WhatsApp Support:** Integrated "Expert Review" button for human verification and payment support.

---

## 🚀 How It Works

1.  **Scan:** Point your camera at a physical auction sheet or upload a screenshot from your gallery.
2.  **Analyze:** Our AI engine parses the Japanese handwritten notes and diagrams.
3.  **Result:** Get a simplified "Executive Summary" and an "Alert/Success" verdict based on the car's condition.
4.  **Verify:** Cross-check the chassis number with live auction data.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Tailwind CSS), JavaScript (ES6+).
* **AI Engine:** Groq Cloud Llama-3 Vision API for OCR and analysis.
* **Imaging:** html2canvas for generating shareable reports.
* **Deployment:** GitHub Pages / Vercel.
